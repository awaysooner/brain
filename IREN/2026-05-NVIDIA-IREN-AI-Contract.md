# NVIDIA + IREN AI Infrastructure Deal

**Date:** May 2026  
**Source:** Public announcements & market reports

## Deal Overview

- **Value**: ~$3.4 billion five-year AI cloud contract
- **Scope**: Deployment of up to **5GW** of AI infrastructure
- **Technology**: NVIDIA DSX AI factory designs + Blackwell GPUs
- **Strategic Significance**: Major validation of IREN’s transition from Bitcoin mining into AI cloud computing

## Key Terms

- IREN will supply GPU capacity (Blackwell) for NVIDIA’s internal AI and research workloads
- NVIDIA receives 5-year warrants to purchase up to **30 million IREN shares** at $70 per share (potential $2.1B investment)

## Implications for IREN

- Accelerates IREN’s pivot toward AI/HPC infrastructure
- Provides long-term revenue visibility
- Strengthens relationship with NVIDIA as both customer and strategic partner

## Notes

This deal was widely viewed as a strong positive signal for IREN’s AI cloud strategy.
