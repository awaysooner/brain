1. Company Overview

Iris Energy (IREN) is a vertically integrated Bitcoin mining and AI cloud infrastructure company. The company owns and operates data centers with significant power capacity, transitioning from pure Bitcoin mining to high-margin AI cloud services. Key assets include ~5 GW of secured power capacity across multiple sites in North America and Australia.
