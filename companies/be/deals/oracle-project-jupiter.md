## Summary

Bloom Energy was selected as the sole power provider for Oracle’s massive 2.45 GW grid-independent AI campus (Project Jupiter) in New Mexico. This replaces previously planned combustion turbines and diesel generators entirely with Bloom fuel cell servers. The win is part of an expanded relationship of up to 2.8 GW and includes Oracle’s ~$400M equity investment in Bloom (Oct 2025). Deployments achieved in 55 days against a 90-day target, demonstrating Bloom’s speed-to-power advantage for hyperscale AI loads.

This is one of the strongest validations to date of Bloom’s onsite, rapid-deploy model for AI infrastructure where traditional grid or legacy generation timelines are unacceptable.
