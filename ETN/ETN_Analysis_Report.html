1. Company Overview

Eaton Corporation is a global leader in power management technologies. The company operates across four main segments:

  • Electrical Americas & Global – Power distribution, switchgear, UPS, and data center infrastructure
  • Aerospace – Hydraulic and electrical systems for commercial and military aircraft
  • Vehicle – Powertrain and drivetrain components
  • eMobility – EV charging and power electronics

Strong secular exposure to AI data center buildout, grid modernization, and vehicle electrification.
